Step-by-Step Registration Guide

Follow these simple steps to register as a voter using the ECINET App

1

Download ECINET App

Download the official ECINET mobile application from Google Play Store or Apple App Store on your smartphone. Ensure you download the authentic app developed by the Election Commission of India.

2

Select New Voter Registration (Form 6)

Open the ECINET app and navigate to the registration section. Select "New Voter Registration" and choose Form 6. This form is specifically for citizens who are registering to vote for the first time.

3

Fill Personal Details

Enter your personal information accurately including your name (as per Aadhaar), date of birth, gender, address, and contact details. Ensure all information matches your Aadhaar card to avoid processing delays.

4

Upload Aadhaar & Documents

Upload clear scanned copies or photos of your Aadhaar card (front and back), recent passport-size photograph, and proof of address. Ensure documents are readable and meet the specified size requirements.

5

Submit & Track Application

Review all entered information carefully, accept the declaration, and submit your application. You will receive an acknowledgment number via SMS. Use this number to track your application status through the app or the official website.

âœ… Eligibility Criteria

To register as a voter, you must be: (1) A citizen of India, (2) At least 18 years of age on the qualifying date, (3) Ordinarily resident of the constituency where you wish to register, and (4) Not disqualified under the Representation of the People Act, 1950.

About Electoral Literacy Club (ELC)

Empowering young voters through education and awareness

Role of ELC

  • Promote voter awareness among youth and students
  • Educate citizens about electoral processes and rights
  • Organize workshops and awareness campaigns
  • Encourage informed and ethical voting practices
  • Build a culture of active democratic participation

ELC Activities

  • School and college voter registration drives
  • Mock parliamentary sessions and debates
  • Street plays and awareness rallies
  • Voter education seminars and webinars
  • Digital literacy campaigns for online registration

Youth Participation

  • First-time voter registration assistance
  • Student volunteer programs for elections
  • Youth ambassador initiatives
  • Campus-to-community outreach programs
  • Democratic values education

Voter Awareness Initiatives

Government programs promoting electoral participation and literacy

SVEEP Program

  • Systematic Voters' Education and Electoral Participation
  • Flagship program of Election Commission of India
  • Multi-media voter awareness campaigns
  • Special focus on urban and youth voters
  • Engaging diverse stakeholders in electoral process

Electoral Literacy Campaigns

  • National Voters' Day celebrations (January 25)
  • Door-to-door voter education drives
  • Community-based awareness programs
  • Regional language voter education materials
  • Accessible voting information for all citizens

Youth Voter Drives

  • Campus voter registration initiatives
  • Digital voter awareness on social media
  • Youth icon engagement programs
  • Student ambassador schemes
